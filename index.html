About SPORYSH

At a Glance

FAQ

Common Questions

What types of herbal raw materials do you supply?

We supply a wide range of dried herbal raw materials including herbs (peppermint, oregano, thyme), leaves (sage, nettle, birch), flowers (chamomile, calendula, linden), roots (valerian, echinacea), berries (rosehip, hawthorn, elder), and other plant parts such as barks, buds, and shoots. All products are sourced from ecologically clean regions of Ukraine.

Do you work with small-volume orders?

We are a B2B supplier focused on wholesale volumes. Minimum order quantities depend on the specific product. Please contact our export department to discuss your requirements and we'll find the best solution for your needs.

What quality certifications do your products have?

Our products comply with ISO 22000 (Food Safety), GMP (Good Manufacturing Practice), HACCP standards, and European Organic certification. We continuously maintain and improve our quality management systems to meet the highest European market requirements.

Can you provide custom cut fractions?

Yes, we offer various cut fractions depending on the product and customer requirements. Common options include whole, coarse cut (TBC 1), medium cut, and fine cut. Specific fraction sizes can be discussed with our team to match your processing needs.

Which countries do you export to?

We primarily export to European Union countries. Our products meet all EU import requirements including phytosanitary certificates, GDPR-compliant documentation, and proper labeling. We handle all export logistics and documentation.
