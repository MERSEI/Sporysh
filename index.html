About Us

From Ukraine to the World

SPORYSH LLC is a Ukrainian supplier of premium herbal raw materials — dried herbs, leaves, flowers, roots and berries — collected across Ukraine's cleanest regions and exported to B2B partners worldwide.

We source from 11 collection regions across western and central Ukraine, process to European quality standards, and deliver to partners in Europe, Asia and North America.
