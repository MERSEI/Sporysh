About SPORYSH

At a Glance

SPORYSH LLC is a Ukrainian supplier of premium herbal raw materials, specializing in dried herbs, leaves, flowers, roots, berries, and plant-based products for the European B2B market.

From precision agriculture and climate-smart practices to market access and quality assurance, our work spans the entire agricultural value chain. We partner with farms, agribusinesses, and distributors to deliver measurable, lasting quality in natural products.

With a passion for sustainable farming communities, we work across regions to implement practical solutions that improve productivity, enhance resilience, and promote environmental stewardship.
